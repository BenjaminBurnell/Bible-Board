Features that turn your Bible into a board.
BibleBoard gives you a visual place to lay out Scripture, insights, and worship ideas so you can see how everything connects at a glance.
1. Visual Bible boards
Lay out verses like sticky notes on a canvas. Drag them around, group ideas, and draw connections you can actually see.
2. Notes anchored to Scripture
Type your thoughts, questions, and sermon points right beside the verses they’re about—no more detached notes app.
3. Worship & music planning
